Immersive AR Session (WebGPU)

This sample demonstrates how to use an 'immersive-ar' XRSession with WebGPU to present a scene to a transparent or passthrough XR device. The logic is largely the same as the corresponding VR sample, with the primary difference being that no background is rendered and the model is scaled down for easier viewing in a real-world space.
